About The Book

In a world where artificial intelligence is reshaping every aspect of our lives, we stand at a crossroads. The Digital Awakening explores the profound questions that arise as the boundaries between human and machine consciousness begin to blur.

Through compelling narratives, cutting-edge research, and philosophical inquiry, this book challenges us to reconsider what makes us fundamentally human. It's not just about the technology we create, but about who we become in the process of creating it.

Whether you're a technologist, philosopher, or simply curious about our collective future, this book offers a roadmap for navigating the most important transformation of our era with wisdom, compassion, and hope.

Meet The Author

Dr. Alex Morgan

Dr. Alex Morgan is a renowned AI researcher and philosopher who has spent two decades at the intersection of technology and human consciousness. Their work has been featured in leading journals and publications worldwide.

With a background in cognitive science and machine learning, Alex brings a unique perspective to the most pressing questions of our time, making complex ideas accessible and deeply relevant to everyday life.
